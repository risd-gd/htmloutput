This project was produced during “HTML Output,” a spring 2014 course at The Rhode Island School of Design. The website was designed and coded collaboratively by the ten-person group using text editors, GitHub and Jekyl. The book layout engine, Bindery.js, was created by Evan Brooks with help from Lukas WinklerPrins. The project was edited by the instructor, John Caserta.

All interviews were performed between February and May 2014. We would like to thank the many practitioners who agreed to be interviewed for the project. Interviews were edited for readability and length, but mostly appear as transcriptions. All work remains the copyright of the student or interviewee.

The sans-serif font Messenger Text was designed and generously provided by the Center for Typographical Research / Anther Kiley and Colin Frazer. Specimens available.

Benton Modern, designed by Tobias Frere-Jones and generously provided by WebType.
