I found that almost all of my work is guided by the question: "what is a piece of browser-native media?" This started with some kinetic patterns or prints, taking advantage of the infinitely long, interactive canvas of the browser window.

The pages explore user-animated and curated form generation through the intuitive gesture of scrolling in a web browser.
